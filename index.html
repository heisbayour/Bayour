The Story

About

Bayour is a Nigerian artist from the heart of Yoruba southwest, born between Ogbomosho and Ilesa and shaped by everything in between. His music lives where sweet melody connects with rhythm and a voice that chases the impossible.

He didn't start music with a plan. He started with a feeling, the kind that comes from unrequited love, betrayal worn like a second skin, and the quiet loneliness of being someone nobody fully sees. That pain became melody. That melody became Bayour.

His sound moves between heartbreak, hope and being a loner, between wanting someone and losing them, between God and everything that tests your faith in Him. He makes music the way he feels it, sensational, rhythmic, melody-based, and he wants you to feel exactly what he felt while making it.
